About The Ministry

Defend The Truth Ministries exists to cultivate resilient followers of Christ who can discern, articulate, and embody the gospel faithfully. What began as a small teaching initiative has grown into a collaborative effort producing resources, cohorts, and local service expressions.

We emphasize historic orthodoxy, intellectual rigor seasoned with humble reliance on the Spirit, and holistic formation that integrates belief, character, and mission.
